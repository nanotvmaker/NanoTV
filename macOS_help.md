# NanoTV macOS Help

Welcome to NanoTV for macOS. This guide will help you get started and master the advanced features of our desktop version.

> 🇨🇳 [简体中文版](macOS_help.zh-CN.md)

---

## Quick Start

### How to Add Your First Subscription
1. Click the **Add** button on the home page.
2. In the **Network Link** window, enter the **Subscription Name** and **Subscription URL**.
3. Click **Add** to submit.

### How to Play Channels
- Click **Subscriptions** in the sidebar, or use <kbd>⇧⌘N</kbd>.
- Double-click a channel or click the Play button to start playback.
- **Quick Play**: Use <kbd>⌃1</kbd> – <kbd>⌃5</kbd> for recent channels.

---

## AI Features

### Real-time Transcription & Translation
1. **Initialize**: Go to **Settings → Subtitles** and click **Initialize Model** (takes about 1-2 minutes on first run).
2. **Enable**: Toggle **Start Real-time Subtitles** after initialization.
3. **During Playback**: Click the **Real-time Subtitles** button at the bottom of the screen, or press <kbd>⌃T</kbd>.
4. **Translation Settings**: Verify Google or Microsoft service in **Settings → Translation Service Configuration**.

### Automatic EPG Translation
- Enable **EPG Translation** in **Settings → EPG**. Currently supports English → Chinese.

---

## Sync & Settings

### iCloud Synchronization
- Enable **iCloud Sync** in **Settings → iCloud** to sync subscriptions and favorites across macOS, iOS, and tvOS.
